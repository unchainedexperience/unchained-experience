Who We Are

Rooted in Kenya, Reaching Beyond It

Unchained Experience is a Nairobi-based tours, travel and flight booking company. We started with a simple belief — that the best trips come from people who actually know the ground, the roads, the parks, and the right contact at the border post.

Today we plan safaris across Kenya's most iconic and lesser-known destinations, arrange cross-border overland routes into Tanzania, Rwanda and Uganda, and book the flights — domestic and international — that connect it all.
